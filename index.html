About

Thor is the God of Thunder in Norse mythology. He is now portrayed in modern media, specifically in the Avenges. Thor has a magic hammer that he uses to fight giants. His magic hammer is named Mjolnir, which enables him to fly. He grew up with his adopted brother Loki and they always had a lot of fun together. Since Loki was adopted, he had special powers that Thor didn't have. So Loki would play little games on Thor.
